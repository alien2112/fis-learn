# Course Worlds (Programming, Graphic Design, Trading)

Goal: switching categories should feel like entering a new culture, visually and linguistically (English + Egyptian Arabic slang).

---

## WORLD 1: Programming

### Concept
The Neon Logic Lab: a futuristic research lab where every lesson feels like unlocking a new system power.

### Mood & Emotion
Focused, sharp, in-control. "I can build this. I can break it. I can fix it."

### Visual Style
- Color palette: `#05070D` (ink), `#00E5FF` (cyan), `#7CFF6B` (ion green), `#FFDD57` (signal amber), `#E7ECF7` (cold light)
- Typography style: geometric sci-fi sans for headlines (`Space Grotesk`), monospace for code/UI numbers (`IBM Plex Mono`), Arabic companion (`IBM Plex Sans Arabic`)
- UI motion & layout style: strict modular grid, "component" cards, code-preview tiles, typing-cursor reveals, scanline hovers, progress states that feel like compile/build steps

### English Copy
- Main Headline: **The Lab Where Ideas Compile.**
- CTA Button: **Run Your First Script**
- Microcopy tone: crisp, command-like, confident; short verbs, zero fluff, "systems mindset"

### Egyptian Arabic Slang Copy
- Main Headline: **هنا هتبني دماغ مبرمج… مش بس كود.**
- CTA Button: **شغّل أول سكريبت**
- Microcopy tone: لهجة "مهندس لاب"؛ أوامر سريعة، ثقة، هزار خفيف، ومصطلحات تكنيكال عادي

## WORLD 2: Graphic Design

### Concept
The Chaos Studio: a loud, messy creative space where you remix, break rules, and discover your signature.

### Mood & Emotion
Playful confidence, freedom, surprise. "Try it. Twist it. Own it."

### Visual Style
- Color palette: `#F6F0E6` (paper), `#101010` (ink), `#FF4D5A` (punch coral), `#2A4BFF` (cobalt), `#B8FF2C` (acid lime)
- Typography style: expressive editorial serif for headlines (`Fraunces`) + clean modern sans for body (`Manrope`), Arabic with personality (`Changa` for headings, `Cairo` or `IBM Plex Sans Arabic` for body)
- UI motion & layout style: asymmetric collage layout, overlapping cards, sticker tags, torn-paper edges, grain textures; elastic drags, "tape peel" reveals, playful hover jitters

### English Copy
- Main Headline: **Turn Chaos into a Signature.**
- CTA Button: **Drop a Poster**
- Microcopy tone: bold, artistic, a little rebellious; invites experimentation and remixing

### Egyptian Arabic Slang Copy
- Main Headline: **اعكّها… وطلّعها شيك.**
- CTA Button: **ارمي أول بوستر**
- Microcopy tone: لهجة "صاحب الاستوديو"؛ خفيفة ودمها خفيف، تشجيع من غير مثالية، وكلام كرييتف طبيعي

## WORLD 3: Trading

### Concept
The Market Arena: a high-stakes pit + command room where discipline beats noise, and speed comes from control.

### Mood & Emotion
Adrenaline with calm authority. "No panic. No ego. Just process."

### Visual Style
- Color palette: `#0B0F14` (charcoal), `#121A24` (slate), `#FF6A00` (signal orange), `#2FD3FF` (electric ice), `#E8EEF6` (clean light)
- Typography style: condensed, punchy headlines (`Saira Condensed`) + numeric/mono for charts (`Space Mono`), Arabic built for clarity (`IBM Plex Sans Arabic`)
- UI motion & layout style: dashboard layout, sharp edges, fast snap transitions; controlled pulses for "market movement", minimal bounce, micro-animations that feel like ticks and confirmations

### English Copy
- Main Headline: **Calm Hands. Sharp Moves.**
- CTA Button: **Run a Demo Trade**
- Microcopy tone: direct, intense, no-hype; emphasizes rules, risk, repetition, control

### Egyptian Arabic Slang Copy
- Main Headline: **إيدك ثابتة… وقرارك محسوب.**
- CTA Button: **جرّب صفقة ديمو**
- Microcopy tone: لهجة "كابتن الحلبة"؛ حادّة وهادية، من غير هبد، تركيز على الخطة وإدارة المخاطر
